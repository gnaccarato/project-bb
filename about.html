History

The Maritime Inn opened almost 10 years ago by a local couple. Their misison was to provide guests with the ability to come and see one of the most amazing regions of Canada, beautiful Prince Edward Island.

With stunning views of scenery and nature, this historic region in the Maritimes provides guests a peaceful and breathtaking experience of true Canadian beauty.

Providing excellent service to all guests, The Maritime Inn is a must see holiday spot for those looking for a unique travel experience. It is no question why this is a favourite bed and breakfast destination.
